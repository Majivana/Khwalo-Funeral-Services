Key Policies

Understanding Your Coverage

6-Month Waiting Period

Must pay premiums for 6 months before coverage activates. Unnatural death covered immediately after first premium.

No Duplicate Coverage

Members cannot be covered by multiple policies. Only the first active policy will be honored.

Child Coverage

Covered until age 21, or 25 if still studying. Newborns must be added within 1 month.

Suicide Clause

Suicidal death covered after 24 months of continuous premium payments.

Important: Three months of missed payments results in automatic cancellation. Rejoining requires a new 6-month waiting period. No refunds for stopped policies.
